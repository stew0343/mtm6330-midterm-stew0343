The Venue

Lago Bar | Grill | View

1001 Queen Elizabeth Driveway, Ottawa ON
613.235.5246
www.lagobargrill.com

From the airport:

Take Airport Pkwy northbound until it turns into Bronson Ave. Turn left on Carling Ave, then left on Preston St and Lago is at the end of the road.

From the east:

Take the highway 417-W until the Bronson exit. Turn left on Bronson Ave, and then right on Carling Ave. Turn left on Preston St and Lago is at the end of the road.

From the west:

Take the highway 417-E until the Rochester exit. Turn right on Rochester St, and then right on Carling Ave. Turn left on Preston St and Lago is at the end of the road.

From the south:

Take Prince of Wales Dr(Route 73 N) into the city. Lago is on the right side of the road once you reach Dow's Lake.

Transit:

The Carling O-Train stop is a 5 minute walk from Lago. You can also take the #85 or #101 bus and get off at the Carling/Preston stop.

Parking:

Parking is located across the street in the paid public lot. Grab a ticket on the way into the lot, and get your parking validated before you leave for the night.
